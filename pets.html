Meet Lily and Riker: Spoiled but not spoiled rotten.

Pitbulls and other bully type breeds...

are often maligned in media, with many misrepresentations surrounding them. For one, their jaws do not lock - no dogs jaws are capable of that, to be frank. This is just one of several silly myths surrounding these loveable cuddle bugs.

As for how dangerous they might be, Lily and Riker are two of the sweetest bully-type pups you will ever meet. Lily can be bought with treats, for one. And, for the record, Riker is terrified of his own farts.

If you'd like to learn more about Pit Bulls and other Bully Breed Types:

  • Visit: Villalobos Rescue Center.
  • Or: 8 Pit Bull Facts Every Dog Lover Should Know.
  • You may be surprised to find that Bully Breeds rank highly in terms of temperament, according to the American Temperament Test Society, INC.
