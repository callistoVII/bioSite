This website is meant to be a fun little biographical study site, dedicated to a very stellar human being.

Welcome to the Home of The Little Medic.

Kara Kitchen is a full-time Paramedic, and a recent transplant to Pennsylvania by way of Florida. She is an auto-didact who taught herself how to operate a sewing machine and has since created many period accurate articles of clothing more at home in the Civil war or Renaissance era than in modern times, and has also sold many custom pieces as well. The very definition of "where there's a will, there's a way," she has also figured out how to make actual pleated kilts. All of which she sales via her e-commerce storefront housed with Etsy.

If that wasn't bonkers enough, Kara also knows her way around a kitchen and does amazing things with carbs and fruit filling. Her artisanal pies are works of art, in and of themselves.

When she is not saving lives, or making the rest of us look bad, she can be found snuggled up with her two pups, Riker and Lily, reading a good book, or off on site-seeing adventures with her husband, Tom.
