Welcome to The Little Medic.

This is a website dedicated to the life, times, and hobbies of Kara Kitchen. Kara is one of the most amazing human beings I have ever had the privilege to know. She is a paramedic, she is a fantastic seamstress, and she does amazing things with carbs.

Due to her tiny stature, Kara earned the nickname "Little Medic" in the beginning stages of her career. She has since gone on to make that name an ironic one, sort of like Little John from Robinhood, only it's her spirit that's mighty. She's been in the medical field since 2009, and has traveled the world, with more plans in the future to visit other far off places. Besides being talented with carbs and threads, she's also very clever with home renovation projects.
