Kitchen Pies Recipe Book

It all started with a birthday offering of a homemade Razzleberry pie.

Kara's father is not a cake person. He preferred pie. For his birthday one year, Kara decided to make a pie from scratch as a gift, rather than buying from the freezer section. Thus began a new passion, that blossomed into a side hustle. Kara has made hundreds of pies since, and even made the pies featured for dessert at her own wedding. Her signature pie remains the Razzleberry, but she is also known for her Key Lime, Pumpkin, and Apple Pies. Her pie crusts are works of art.

"After several years of referring to my own recipes and, of course, well-used and loved recipes from my many (many, many... many...) baking cook books, I decided to make my own pie baking cook book. It contains all my recipes, a lot of photos (I still haven't gotten around to making ALL of them...), and a not small amount of snark. I think that all recipe books should contain some witticisms in them."

- Kara Rush, on the making of her own cookbook.
